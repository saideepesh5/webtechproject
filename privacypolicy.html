Smart Scholars Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 8, 2023

Smart Scholars is committed to protecting the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share your personal information.

What information do we collect?

We collect personal information from you when you create an account on our website, enroll in a course, or purchase content. This information may include your name, email address, password, and payment information. We may also collect information about your use of our website, such as the courses you enroll in, the pages you visit, and the resources you download.

How do we use your information?

We use your personal information to provide you with our services, improve our website, and communicate with you about your account and our products and services. We may also use your information to personalize your experience on our website and to send you promotional emails.

How do we share your information?

We do not share your personal information with any third parties, except as required by law or as necessary to provide you with our services. For example, we may share your payment information with our payment processor to process your payments.

How do we protect your information?

We take all reasonable steps to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. We use secure servers and encryption to protect your data. We also require our employees to maintain the confidentiality of your information.

Your choices

You have the right to access, review, and correct your personal information. You also have the right to opt out of receiving promotional emails from us. You can exercise these rights by contacting us at [email protected]

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make any changes, we will post the updated Privacy Policy on our website.
